Hi! I'm Casey - the one that'll be bringing your surf brand to life in analog.

Sure, there's a thousand ways to capture a product, an event, or a session - but the lure of analog is undeniably magical. It's why I started using film 8 years ago and never looked back. And who doesn't want to feel like they're jumping into a time machine while jumping into their new wetsuit?

When you market in analog, you remind your audience of how this industry all began and how they can play a part in the larger surf story too. It's my job to find your unique sidequest in that story and create a set of images and videos that breathe life into it. It's the most rewarding thing, placing what was once just an idea, into the backdrop of a vintage ocean fairytale with your brand's unique fingerprint all over it.
Suddenly your brand stands in its own right as something your audience can visualize immediately. Whether you go for sunset colors and a blurred longboard tropical vibe, or stormy hues with more masculine shapes - let's figure out what your story is and tell it right.

Ready to get started? Let's channel my years of film storytelling experience and personal surfing experience into a visual story that will make your brand stand out.

P.S. If digital is more your thing, or you want to sprinkle some between your film shots, I've got you there too.
